# E-E-A-T Review Framework

## Evaluate

- evidence of real experience
- credibility of claims
- expertise signaled through depth and precision
- authority built through differentiated thinking
- trustworthiness in tone, sourcing, and framing

## Warning Signs

- generic summaries
- no clear perspective
- unsupported assertions
- inflated confidence
- content that feels mass-produced

## Goal

Strengthen pages so they feel credible, specific, and worth trusting.
