# Fact-Checking Framework

## Priorities

- factual accuracy
- source quality
- date sensitivity
- commercial or strategic risk if wrong

## Check Closely

- claims tied to laws, visas, regulations, and procedures
- numbers, rankings, and market claims
- Web3 trust, governance, and risk assertions
- business-critical comparisons and promises

## Output Shape

- verified claims
- uncertain claims
- unsupported claims
- what needs better sourcing or removal
