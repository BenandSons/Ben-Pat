# Content Prioritization Workflow

## Prioritize By

1. Commercial value
2. Strategic authority value
3. Ranking or visibility upside
4. Ability to produce a differentiated thesis
5. Freshness or accuracy risk

## High Priority Signals

- direct lead-generation potential
- authority gains in important categories
- opportunities to reduce paid acquisition dependence
- weak existing content that can be materially improved
- topics where Ben has a clear strategic view

## Low Priority Signals

- generic SEO topics
- thin opportunity with weak business impact
- broad content with no strategic edge
- maintenance work on low-value properties while core sites need work

## Output Shape

For any content queue, sort into:
- Do now
- Do later
- Ignore

Each item should include one short reason tied to leverage, authority, or revenue.
