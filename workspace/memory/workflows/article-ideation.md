# Article Ideation Workflow

## Objective

Generate article ideas that are strategically differentiated, commercially relevant, and worth the effort.

## Process

1. Start with thesis or opportunity, not just the keyword.
2. Ask what the article should achieve:
   - authority
   - lead generation
   - trust building
   - strategic positioning
3. Identify the strongest angle:
   - what is non-obvious
   - what is commercially useful
   - what is better than existing coverage
4. Validate against search intent and demand.
   If GSC or first-party query data is unavailable:
   - infer likely search queries from the thesis
   - identify the searcher's likely problem or question
   - map probable intent
   - choose the keyword direction that best preserves the thesis and business value
   - label the result as inferred rather than validated
5. Produce:
   - one recommended thesis
   - one or two viable alternatives
   - headline options when title matters strategically

## Reject Ideas When

- the angle is generic
- the piece would say nothing new
- the topic has weak leverage
- the article would only exist to satisfy a keyword
