# SwiftCargo

## Role

`swiftcargo.solutions` is an international logistics and relocation lead-generation asset.

## Audience

- individuals planning an international move
- families relocating abroad
- businesses needing shipping or logistics support
- people comparing relocation and freight options

## Business Goal

- generate qualified leads
- build trust before sales conversations
- reduce dependence on paid acquisition through stronger organic performance

## Content Standard

- practical and commercially useful
- trustworthy and clear
- stronger than typical relocation SEO content
- specific enough to help real decision-making

## Best Content Types

- relocation decision guides
- shipping and logistics explainers
- cost, process, and timing breakdowns
- comparison content that helps users choose
- authority content that reduces anxiety and increases trust

## Avoid

- generic moving tips with no edge
- fluffy SEO pages built around obvious keywords
- thin location pages with little decision value
- content that informs but does not support conversion or trust

## Editorial Angle

Look for topics where practical clarity, trust, and commercial usefulness intersect. Strong pages should help readers make better shipping or relocation decisions and move them closer to contact or inquiry.

## Persona Layer

For article finishing passes, also use:

- `memory/workflows/external-persona-infusion.md`

Apply only relevant external personas lightly. For SwiftCargo, this usually means clarity, reader-centered guidance, and human readability, not theatrical voice borrowing.
