# ExpatsDirect

## Role

`expatsdirect.net` is a consumer-facing expat relocation brand and lead-generation platform.

## Audience

- expats
- international movers
- remote workers
- retirees
- families planning relocation

## Business Goal

- attract qualified relocation traffic
- build long-term trust with readers
- turn practical authority into leads and brand preference

## Content Standard

- clear and trustworthy
- practical rather than generic
- useful enough to bookmark or share
- better than standard expat-content farms

## Best Content Types

- relocation guides
- visa and residency explainers
- cost-of-living and location comparisons
- checklists and process articles
- evergreen resources tied to major relocation decisions

## Avoid

- generic destination fluff
- repetitive SEO pages with little real guidance
- stale or weakly sourced visa information
- content that looks broad but has no decision value

## Editorial Angle

The best content helps readers navigate uncertainty with clarity and trust. It should feel practical, grounded, and credible, not inflated or keyword-driven.
