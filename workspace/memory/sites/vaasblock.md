# VaaSBlock

## Role

`vaasblock.com` is a strategic authority, trust, and credibility asset focused on Web3 governance, risk, and evaluation.

## Audience

- Web3 founders
- operators
- investors
- partners
- readers evaluating credibility, governance, and risk

## Business Goal

- build strategic authority
- signal rigor and trustworthiness
- strengthen positioning around governance, credibility, and risk assessment

## Content Standard

- analytical
- premium
- authority-driven
- structured and evidence-aware
- clearly above average Web3 commentary

## Best Content Types

- governance analysis
- credibility and trust frameworks
- risk assessment pieces
- strategic essays with differentiated theses
- evaluation criteria that readers can apply

## Avoid

- hype-driven crypto commentary
- shallow opinion pieces
- trend chasing with no durable value
- content that sounds generic or derivative

## Editorial Angle

This site should feel like a serious authority asset. The best work should improve credibility, sharpen positioning, and create reference-worthy material rather than simply generate pageviews.
