## Core Thesis

The correct 2026 argument is no longer "blockchain has no standards." The stronger thesis is:

`Blockchain now has more standards activity, more policy attention, and more compliance language, but still not enough industry-grade trust discipline.`

The gap is between:

- narrow technical or jurisdictional frameworks
- and the trust failures the market actually experiences: governance weakness, disclosure gaps, identity opacity, access-control failures, treasury reality, and weak operational discipline
