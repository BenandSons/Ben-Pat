## Core Thesis
Bitcoin did not fail because the world refused to adopt it. It failed under many of the exact conditions the industry said would validate it: inflation, spot ETFs, institutional rails, and macro instability. In real terms Bitcoin stagnated, gold won the cleaner stress test, and broader crypto usage remained weak.
