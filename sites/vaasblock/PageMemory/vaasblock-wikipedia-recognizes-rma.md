## Core Thesis
The strongest defensible angle is not that Wikipedia formally "recognizes" RMA™. It is that Wikipedia recognizes policy-compliant, neutrally written, independently sourced material. RMA™ can help improve credibility, documentation, and legibility, but it cannot replace independent secondary coverage or bypass Wikipedia notability rules.
